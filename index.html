Our story

We opened in May 2010, and our bread has been loved

in the neighborhood. We use only selected ingredients

and be commited to trustworthy products.


Croissants are our flagship product, which made from

French butter, freshly milled organic flours, and long

fermentation periods.


To serve a more delightful experience to oue customers,

we opend a resturant next to the bakery in July 2018.

Our meals are inspired by the seasons and use fresh

fruits and vegetables fresh from the farmers' market.
